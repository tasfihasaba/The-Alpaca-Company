Employee Benefits




Our company is a diverse and inclusive environment for our employees.

We offer benefits and opportunities to ensure that our employees can efficiently

do their tasks and allow for open space for communication.

Some of the benefits we offer are:

- Floating holidays and unlimited PTO

- Leave for all parents

- Family building benefits

- Gender affirming benefits

- Diverse healthcare providers

- Tuition assistance and financial education

- Employee Resource Groups

DEI




It's been proven time and time again that a diverse, equitable, and inclusive (DEI)

organization is more successful in the marketplace. For Alpaca, diversity is also the right

thing to do. We make a daily commitment to ensure you feel valued, welcomed,

and respected for your individuality, experiences, and perspective. Our Employee

Resource Groups (ERGs) are designed to build community, raise awareness,

increase understanding, promote positive change, and influence the business. As an

ERG member, you can find engagement, share ideas, and support others.
